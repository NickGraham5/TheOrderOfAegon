About Aegon's Order

Our Clan's Story.

  • 2018

    Our Humble Beginnings

    In 2018, a guardian named Aegon introduced some former Overwatch players to a game called Destiny 2. Soon they had forsaken all other video games and were rushing home each night to grind on them light levels.

  • 2018

    The Orphans find a Foster Home

    In need of Powerful Engrams, Aegon and his new recruits joined the order of Toland. The relationship proved mutually beneficial, but leadership was weak. Turmoil was brewing.

  • 2018

    A Mutiny Afoot

    One cold December night, Aegon and his fellow guardians gathered round many pitchers of Ale, and let their frustrations boil over. They sent ravens to Toland, taunting him. Threatening a takeover. Toland's throne would not be his for long.

  • March 2019

    A New Dawn

    At the conclusion of season 5, Aegon made his move. With his trusted Guardians by his side, Aegon seceded from Toland's Order and founded the mighty Aegon's Order. Aegon's Order grows stronger each day, and needs YOU to join its ranks, Guardian.
